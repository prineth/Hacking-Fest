<h2> What is a phishing attak ?</h2>
<p> Phishing is a type of social engineering attack often used to steal user data, including login credentials and credit card numbers. 
It occurs when an attacker, masquerading as a trusted entity, dupes a victim into opening an email, instant message, or text message. </p>
